Welcome to Three Card Money Monte.

Game Description

A card game which tests your Memory. There are three cards in the deck including the Ace of spades , the Card is shown, a bet is placed, then the card is shuffled. the Player must select the right card to win, or else they lose their money.

Rules of the game.

  • Place your bets.
  • Follow the position of the Ace of Spades.
  • Click the Start Game button to shuffle the card.
  • Click your winning card.
